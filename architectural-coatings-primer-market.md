<p><p>The architectural coatings primer market is segmented into various types, notably waterproof and corrosion protection primers. Waterproof primers are designed to create a barrier that prevents moisture penetration, making them ideal for areas exposed to water, such as bathrooms and exteriors. Corrosion protection primers are formulated to inhibit rust and deterioration on metal surfaces, ensuring longevity and durability in structures like bridges and industrial facilities. Both types enhance the performance of topcoats, ensuring optimal adhesion and extending the life of painted surfaces.</p></p>

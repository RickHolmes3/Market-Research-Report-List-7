<p><ul><li>Common Bias Tyre</li><li>Bias Belted Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diagonal tire markets consist primarily of common bias tires and bias belted tires. Common bias tires have plies that crisscross at angles, offering flexibility and a softer ride, making them suitable for various applications like agriculture and off-road vehicles. Bias belted tires, on the other hand, incorporate a belt layer under the tread, enhancing stability and reducing sidewall flex. These tires provide improved handling and are commonly used in passenger vehicles and light trucks, balancing durability with performance.</p></p>

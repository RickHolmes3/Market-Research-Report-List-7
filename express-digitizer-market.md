<p><p>The Express Digitizer market encompasses various interface types, including PXI, PCI, PCIe, PXIe, and USB. PXI provides a modular platform for automated testing, while PCI and PCIe offer high-speed data transfer directly on the motherboard for embedded applications. PXIe enhances PCIe with synchronization and timing features, ideal for complex test setups. USB interfaces facilitate easy connectivity and portability for digitizers, making them suitable for less demanding applications. Each type serves distinct needs in data acquisition and processing environments.</p></p>

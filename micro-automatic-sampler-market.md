<p><p>The Micro Automatic Sampler market can be categorized into three main types: Inhaled Type, Pusher Type, and Monolithic Sample Ring Type. Inhaled Type samplers facilitate the collection of gases or aerosols, ideal for environmental and pharmaceutical applications. Pusher Type samplers mechanically extract samples from a variety of media, enhancing precision and efficiency. Monolithic Sample Ring Type utilizes a single continuous structure to capture samples, ensuring consistent delivery and minimizing contamination, making it suitable for various analytical processes.</p></p>

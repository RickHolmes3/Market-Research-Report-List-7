<p><ul><li>Tooth-Supported</li><li>Implant-Supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implant overdenture market is primarily divided into two types: tooth-supported and implant-supported. Tooth-supported overdentures use existing natural teeth as anchors, providing stability while preserving the jawbone and maintaining some natural tooth structure. In contrast, implant-supported overdentures rely entirely on dental implants, which are surgically placed in the jawbone for superior support and stability. This type often leads to enhanced function and comfort, making it a preferred option for individuals with significant tooth loss or inadequate remaining teeth.</p></p>

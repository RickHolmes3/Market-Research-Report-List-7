<p><strong>The External Pacemakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The external pacemaker market features two primary types: single chamber pacemakers and dual chamber pacemakers. </p><p>Single chamber pacemakers regulate one heart chamber, typically the right ventricle, providing essential support for patients with bradycardia. In contrast, dual chamber pacemakers manage both the atrium and ventricle, enhancing coordination between chambers and improving cardiac efficiency. These devices cater to different patient needs, with single chamber devices being simpler and often less invasive, while dual chamber models provide a more comprehensive solution for complex cardiac conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>The stainless steel honeycomb core market is categorized into two main types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer stainless steel honeycomb cores consist of a single layer of honeycomb structure, offering lightweight properties and good strength, suitable for applications like aerospace and automotive. In contrast, multilayer stainless steel honeycomb cores are composed of multiple layers, providing enhanced durability and insulation. This type is preferred in construction and industrial applications where additional structural integrity and thermal performance are required.</p></p>

<p><p>The mounted sprayer market encompasses several types designed for different agricultural needs. The 3-point hitch sprayer attaches to tractors, providing stability and versatility. ATV-mounted sprayers offer maneuverability in smaller areas, ideal for gardens and crops. Front-mount sprayers are attached to the front of tractors, allowing for better visibility and access to hard-to-reach spots. Straddle tractor-mounted sprayers are designed for row crops, enabling precise application between rows, enhancing efficiency and crop protection in agricultural practices.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Dispersion Method</li><li>Suspension Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE grease market consists of two main types based on their preparation methods: dispersion and suspension. The dispersion method involves uniformly distributing fine PTFE particles in a lubricant base, enhancing its performance characteristics like low friction and temperature stability. In contrast, the suspension method incorporates larger PTFE particles within the grease, offering superior lubrication and resistance to wear. Both methods cater to diverse applications in industries requiring reliable lubrication solutions, each with unique advantages in performance and functionality.</p></p>

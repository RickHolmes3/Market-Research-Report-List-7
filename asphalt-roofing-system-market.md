<p><p>The asphalt roofing system market comprises three main types: Built-Up Roofing (BUR), Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), and Atactic Polypropylene (APP). BUR consists of multiple layers of asphalt and fabric, providing durability and puncture resistance, ideal for flat roofs. SBS roofing incorporates rubber-based modifiers, enhancing flexibility and temperature resistance, suitable for various climates. APP features a polypropylene-based modifier, offering excellent UV resistance and long-lasting performance, making it ideal for commercial applications. Each type caters to specific roofing needs and conditions.</p></p>

Electrostatic sprayers utilize charged particles to evenly coat surfaces, ensuring comprehensive coverage and effective disinfection, often reaching difficult areas. Non-electrostatic sprayers operate without this charge, relying on traditional spray mechanisms, which may require more effort for even application. Both types are essential for maintaining sanitized environments across various sectors, including healthcare, hospitality, and retail, each offering unique advantages depending on the specific disinfection needs.</p></p>

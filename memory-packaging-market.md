<p><p>The Memory Packaging Market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Flip-chip**: This technology allows for high-density interconnection by flipping the chip and connecting it directly to the substrate, enhancing performance and reducing size.</p><p>2. **Lead-frame**: A cost-effective option that uses a metal frame to hold the semiconductor and connect it with external circuits, suitable for various applications.</p><p>3. **Through-Silicon Via (TSV)**: This method enables vertical connections through the silicon chip, facilitating high-density stacking and improved performance.</p><p>4. **Others**: This category encompasses emerging and specialized packaging techniques, catering to niche applications and evolving technologies.</p></p>

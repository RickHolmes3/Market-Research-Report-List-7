<p><p>The C5 hydrogenated resin market is primarily segmented into two types: monomer and copolymer. Monomer resins are derived from a single type of chemical building block, providing consistent properties and performance, making them suitable for applications like adhesives and coatings. In contrast, copolymer resins are created from two or more different monomers, resulting in enhanced versatility and performance characteristics. This diversity allows copolymers to be tailored for specific applications, such as sealants or rubber modifiers, catering to varying industry demands.</p></p>

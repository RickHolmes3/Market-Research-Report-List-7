<p><p>The blood dialysis machines market encompasses several treatment types aimed at managing kidney failure. Hemodialysis utilizes a machine to filter waste from the blood externally, while peritoneal dialysis uses the lining of the abdomen to cleanse the blood internally. Hemofiltration focuses on removing impurities through a different filtration process, often used in intensive care. Other methods may include advanced technologies or hybrid approaches that combine these techniques, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences for renal replacement therapy.</p></p>

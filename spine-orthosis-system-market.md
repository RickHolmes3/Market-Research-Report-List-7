<p><p>The Spine Orthosis System market is categorized into Inlay Type and Externally Assisted systems. Inlay Type orthoses are designed to be integrated within existing support structures, providing enhanced stabilization of the spine. These are often used for mild to moderate conditions. Externally Assisted systems, on the other hand, are wearable devices that offer increased support and immobilization, suitable for severe spinal issues or post-surgical recovery. Both types aim to improve patient mobility and comfort while promoting spinal healing.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Chip-less RFID Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW</li><li>TFTC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip-less RFID technology utilizes passive components, such as Surface Acoustic Waves (SAW) and Time-Frequency Tagging Communication (TFTC), to enable wireless identification without conventional microchips. SAW tags leverage acoustic waves for data transmission, offering high durability and temperature resistance, ideal for industrial applications. On the other hand, TFTC utilizes time-frequency domains for efficient data encoding, providing robust communication in dynamic environments. Both types promote reduced costs and increased scalability in sectors like logistics, retail, and supply chain management.</p></p>

<p><p>The PVC electrical conduits market comprises two primary types: PVC rigid conduits and PVC flexible conduits. PVC rigid conduits are strong, durable, and typically used in applications requiring high protection and support for electrical wiring, often installed in walls or ceilings. In contrast, PVC flexible conduits offer greater adaptability and are used in areas where flexibility is needed, such as in tight spaces or for equipment that requires movement. Both types provide effective protection against environmental factors and electrical hazards.</p></p>

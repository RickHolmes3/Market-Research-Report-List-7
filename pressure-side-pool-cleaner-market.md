<p><strong>The Pressure-Side Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Booster Pump</li><li>Filter Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure-side pool cleaner market is divided into two main types: booster pump and filter pump cleaners. Booster pump cleaners operate independently from the pool's existing filtration system, using a dedicated booster pump to enhance water flow and improve cleaning efficiency. In contrast, filter pump cleaners utilize the existing pool filtration system, relying on the pool's pump to circulate water and facilitate debris removal. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to varying pool sizes and maintenance needs.</p></p>

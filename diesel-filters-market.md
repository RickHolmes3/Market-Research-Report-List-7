<p><strong>The Diesel Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line Type</li><li>Built-in Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel filters market comprises two primary types: in-line type and built-in type filters. In-line filters are external devices installed in the fuel line, designed to remove contaminants before fuel reaches the engine, providing ease of maintenance and replacement. Built-in filters, on the other hand, are integrated within the fuel system components, offering a compact design and often requiring less space. Both types are crucial for ensuring engine efficiency, performance, and longevity by protecting against fuel contamination.</p></p>

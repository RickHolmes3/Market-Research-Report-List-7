<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) machines are categorized into two primary types: Type I and Type II. Type I CMP machines primarily focus on traditional semiconductor wafer polishing, employing a combination of chemical slurries and mechanical abrasion to achieve a smooth surface finish. Type II machines, on the other hand, are designed for advanced applications, such as polishing larger substrates or specialized materials, featuring enhanced automation and precision control. Both types cater to the growing demands of semiconductor and electronics industries for high-quality surface finishes.</p></p>

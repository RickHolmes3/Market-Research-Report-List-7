<p><p>The pharmaceutical capsule filling equipment market comprises three main types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic equipment offers high efficiency and precision, automating the entire filling process, making them ideal for large-scale production. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, balancing efficiency and operator control, suitable for medium-scale operations. Manual types rely entirely on human labor for filling capsules, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity but are less efficient, making them more appropriate for small-scale or specialized production needs.</p></p>

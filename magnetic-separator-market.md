<p><p>The magnetic separator market comprises various types, including tubular, square, and roller magnetic separators. Tubular magnetic separators are designed for liquid processing, efficiently removing ferrous particles in fluids. Square magnetic separators are often used in bulk material handling, providing effective containment and separation of contaminants from dry products. Roller magnetic separators, ideal for separating fine materials, utilize a rotating magnet to enhance extraction efficiency. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, catering to the diverse needs of metal separation and contamination control.</p></p>

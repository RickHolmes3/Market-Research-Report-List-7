<p><strong>The Flyash Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F</li><li>Class C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fly ash is categorized into two main types: Class F and Class C, each offering distinct properties for construction applications. Class F fly ash, produced from burning anthracite or bituminous coal, has pozzolanic properties, making it ideal for enhancing concrete strength and durability. In contrast, Class C fly ash, derived from lignite and sub-bituminous coal, possesses both pozzolanic and cementitious properties, allowing it to bind with water and contribute to concrete's structural integrity. Both types are widely used in sustainable construction practices.</p></p>

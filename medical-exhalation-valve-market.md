<p><p>The medical exhalation valve market is divided into two primary types: single-use and reusable valves. Single-use valves are designed for one-time applications, ensuring high levels of sterility and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for emergency situations or infection control. Reusable valves, on the other hand, are engineered for multiple uses, often made from durable materials that can withstand repeated sterilization. This type is cost-effective over time and aligns with sustainability efforts in healthcare practices.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Electrochemical</li><li>Optical</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell-based label-free monitoring technologies encompass various methods, including electrochemical, optical, and piezoelectric approaches. Electrochemical methods detect cellular activity by measuring changes in electrical signals. Optical techniques utilize light interaction with cells to monitor biological processes non-invasively. Piezoelectric methods rely on changes in mass or mechanical properties of cells, offering sensitivity to cellular alterations. Together, these technologies enhance real-time analysis of cell behavior, facilitating drug discovery, toxicity studies, and biomarker identification without the need for labeling agents.</p></p>

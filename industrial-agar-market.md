<p><p>The Industrial Agar market comprises various types of agarose, categorized mainly into Standard Melting Agarose, Low Melting Point Agarose, and Others. Standard Melting Agarose is widely used for routine gel electrophoresis and is characterized by its stable melting and gelling properties. Low Melting Point Agarose allows for easier recovery of samples after electrophoresis due to its lower gelation temperature.

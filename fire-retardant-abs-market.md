<p><p>The Fire Retardant ABS market is categorized into two main types: Non-halogen type and Halogen type. Non-halogen fire retardant ABS uses alternative chemical additives that do not contain halogens, making it an environmentally friendly option with lower toxicity during combustion. In contrast, Halogen type fire retardant ABS incorporates halogenated compounds which provide effective flame resistance but can release harmful gases when burned. Both types are utilized in industries requiring fire safety, such as electronics, automotive, and construction.</p></p>

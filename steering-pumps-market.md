<p><p>The steering pumps market consists of different types that cater to various hydraulic steering systems. Blade type steering pumps utilize rotating blades to create pressure, offering smooth operation and efficiency. Gear type steering pumps use rotating gears to generate hydraulic flow, known for their reliability and robustness. Plunger type steering pumps operate using axial movement of plungers, providing high pressure and compact design, ideal for dynamic steering applications. Each type serves distinct automotive needs, enhancing vehicle maneuverability and performance.</p></p>

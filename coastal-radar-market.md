<p><p>The coastal radar market primarily includes three types: X-Band Radar, S-Band Radar, and Other. X-Band Radar operates on shorter wavelengths, providing high-resolution imagery suitable for detecting small vessels and weather phenomena, making it ideal for coastal surveillance. S-Band Radar, with longer wavelengths, excels in long-range detection and the ability to penetrate heavy rain, making it useful for tracking larger vessels and aircraft. Other radar types may include L-Band and frequency-modulated continuous wave radars, catering to specific applications in coastal monitoring and management.</p></p>

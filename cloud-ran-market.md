<p><p>The Cloud RAN market encompasses several critical components. Baseband Units (BBUs) handle signal processing and resource management. Optical Transport Networks (OTNs) facilitate high-speed data transmission between network elements. Processors enhance computational capabilities across the system. Remote Radio Units (RRUs) convert digital signals to radio frequencies, enabling wireless communication. Servers provide the necessary infrastructure for cloud-based operations. Measurement devices ensure network performance and reliability by monitoring various parameters, supporting optimal functionality and service quality across the RAN ecosystem.</p></p>

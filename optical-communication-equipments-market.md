<p><p>The optical communication equipment market comprises several key components: modulators, demodulators, transmitters, receivers, and optical fibers. Modulators and demodulators are essential for converting electrical signals into optical signals and vice versa, facilitating high-speed data transmission. Transmitters generate and send optical signals, while receivers detect and interpret them. Optical fibers serve as the medium for these signals, providing low-loss, high-bandwidth communication pathways. Together, these elements enable efficient data transfer in telecommunications and networking applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The Automotive Throttle Body Assembly market comprises three key components: the actuator, throttle plate, and throttle position sensor. The actuator regulates air intake by controlling the throttle plate's position, determining engine power and efficiency. The throttle plate is the valve that manages airflow into the engine, directly impacting performance. The throttle position sensor detects the plate's position and relays this information to the engine control unit for optimal fuel-air mixture, ensuring responsive acceleration and reduced emissions.</p></p>

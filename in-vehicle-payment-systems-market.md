<p><p>In-Vehicle Payment Systems can be categorized into three main types: Embedded Systems, Mooring Systems, and Integrated Systems. </p><p>Embedded Systems are built directly into the vehicle, allowing seamless transactions for tolls and parking. Mooring Systems enable payments via dedicated kiosks or docking stations for services like charging or parking. Integrated Systems combine vehicle interfaces with mobile applications, facilitating transactions across multiple platforms and enhancing user convenience. Each system offers unique advantages, addressing different consumer needs within the transportation ecosystem.</p></p>

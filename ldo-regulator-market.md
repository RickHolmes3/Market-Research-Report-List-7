<p><p>An LDO (Low-Dropout) regulator is a type of voltage regulator that maintains a constant output voltage despite variations in input voltage and load conditions. Unlike traditional linear regulators, LDOs require a lower voltage difference between the input and output, making them ideal for battery-powered devices where efficiency is crucial. Their simplicity, compact size, and low noise operation contribute to their widespread use in consumer electronics, automotive applications, telecommunications, and industrial equipment.</p><p>The LDO regulator market is projected to experience steady growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 4.70% during the forecast period.

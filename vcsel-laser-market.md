<p><p>The VCSEL (Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser) market is primarily segmented into single-mode and multimode categories. Single-mode VCSELs emit light through a single spatial mode, offering higher accuracy and longer distance communication, commonly used in data centers and high-speed networks. In contrast, multimode VCSELs transmit multiple modes of light, facilitating higher data rates over shorter distances in applications like local area networks and short-range communications. Both types cater to distinct needs in optical communication and various technologies.</p></p>

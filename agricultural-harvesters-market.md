<p><p>The agricultural harvesters market is divided into two main types: walking type and suspension type. In contrast, suspension type harvesters offer enhanced stability and comfort, featuring a suspended design that minimizes ground impact. They are typically used for larger fields, providing efficiency and reducing operator fatigue during prolonged use, catering to diverse agricultural needs and field conditions.</p></p>

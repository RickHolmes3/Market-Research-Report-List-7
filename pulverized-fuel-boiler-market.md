<p><ul><li>Subcritical Pulverized Fuel Boiler</li><li>Supercritical Pulverized Fuel Boiler</li><li>Ultra-Supercritical Pulverized Fuel Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulverized fuel boiler market comprises three main types: subcritical, supercritical, and ultra-supercritical boilers. Subcritical pulverized fuel boilers operate below critical pressure, optimizing energy conversion but with lower efficiency. Supercritical boilers work at or above critical pressure, enhancing thermal efficiency and reducing emissions. Ultra-supercritical boilers exceed supercritical parameters, achieving even higher efficiencies and lower coal consumption, making them more environmentally friendly. Each type offers distinct advantages for power generation, impacting operational costs and environmental performance.</p></p>

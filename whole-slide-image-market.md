<p><p>The Whole Slide Image (WSI) market is primarily categorized into brightfield and fluorescence imaging. Brightfield imaging utilizes standard light to visualize stained tissue samples, enabling pathologists to assess histological features effectively. In contrast, fluorescence imaging employs fluorescent markers to highlight specific cellular components or processes, allowing for more detailed and precise analysis of biological specimens. Both technologies are essential in digital pathology, facilitating remote diagnostics, enhancing workflow efficiency, and improving diagnostic accuracy in various clinical applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The IR (Infrared) Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared (Swir) Imaging</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared (Mwir) Imaging</li><li>Long Wave Infrared (Lwir) Imaging</li><li>Wave Infrared (Fwir) Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared imaging market is segmented into four main types based on wavelength ranges. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) imaging operates between 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, ideal for applications like surveillance and agricultural monitoring. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) imaging, spanning 3 to 5 micrometers, is commonly used in military and industrial applications. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) imaging operates from 8 to 12 micrometers, popular in thermal imaging for building inspections. Finally, Far Infrared (FIR) imaging extends beyond LWIR, focusing on high-temperature applications and advanced scientific research.</p></p>

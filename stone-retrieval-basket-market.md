<p><p>The stone retrieval basket market comprises two primary types: nitinol stone baskets and stainless steel stone baskets. Nitinol baskets, made from a nickel-titanium alloy, offer superior flexibility and memory properties, making them ideal for navigating complex anatomy and retrieving stones of varying sizes. In contrast, stainless steel baskets are known for their strength and durability, providing reliable performance in stone retrieval. Both types cater to the needs of urologists, enhancing the effectiveness of minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>

<p><p>Rigging screws are essential components in various applications, categorized into two main types: open-body and closed-body. Open-body rigging screws feature a visible screw mechanism, allowing for easy inspection and adjustment of tension. They are often preferred for applications where frequent adjustments are necessary. In contrast, closed-body rigging screws have a sealed design, offering enhanced protection against dirt and debris, making them suitable for harsh environments. Both types cater to specific needs in rigging and load-bearing situations.</p></p>

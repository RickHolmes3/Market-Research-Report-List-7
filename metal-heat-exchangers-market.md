<p><p>The metal heat exchangers market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Shell & tube heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes, with one set carrying the heat transfer fluid and the other circulating the coolant, ideal for high-pressure environments. Plate heat exchangers utilize metal plates for efficient heat transfer, suitable for limited spaces. Air-cooled heat exchangers use air to dissipate heat, prevalent in outdoor applications. Other types may include spiral heat exchangers and double-pipe designs, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The defrosting and thawing equipment market is primarily categorized into chamber type and rotary type systems. Chamber type equipment utilizes a closed environment to slowly and evenly thaw food products, ensuring minimal temperature fluctuation. In contrast, rotary type equipment employs a rotating mechanism to facilitate quicker and more uniform defrosting, reducing waiting time and enhancing efficiency. Both types are vital for the food industry, ensuring safe food handling while maintaining product quality during the defrosting process.</p></p>

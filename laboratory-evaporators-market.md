<p><p>The laboratory evaporators market comprises various types, each designed for specific applications. Nitrogen evaporators utilize nitrogen gas to evaporate solvents rapidly, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Vacuum evaporators lower the boiling point by creating a vacuum, suitable for concentrating solutions without thermal degradation. Rotary evaporators facilitate efficient solvent removal through rotation and reduced pressure, often used in chemical and pharmaceutical labs. Other types may include centrifugal or flash evaporators, each serving specialized roles in laboratory settings for sample preparation and concentration.</p></p>

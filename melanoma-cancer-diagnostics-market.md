<p><strong>The Melanoma Cancer Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) Tests</li><li>Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) Tests</li><li>Immunohistochemical (IHC) Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The melanoma cancer diagnostics market includes various testing methods to detect and analyze melanoma cells. Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) tests identify specific genetic mutations associated with melanoma by using fluorescent probes. Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) tests offer a broader genomic analysis, enabling detection of chromosomal abnormalities. Immunohistochemical (IHC) tests assess the presence of specific proteins in melanoma tissues, providing insights into tumor behavior and potential treatment responses. Together, these diagnostics enhance personalized treatment strategies for melanoma patients.</p></p>

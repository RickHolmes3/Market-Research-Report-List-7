<p><p>The pH measuring devices market is primarily classified into two types: pointer and digital display. Pointer devices utilize analog scales, where a needle indicates pH levels, offering simplicity and durability. In contrast, digital display devices provide precise and easily readable measurements through electronic means, often featuring advanced functionalities such as data logging and calibration alerts. While pointer devices are favored for their cost-effectiveness and reliability in basic applications, digital displays are preferred for accuracy and user-friendly interfaces in laboratory and industrial settings.</p></p>

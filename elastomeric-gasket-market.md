<p><p>The elastomeric gasket market primarily comprises two types: rubber gaskets and thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) gaskets. Rubber gaskets, made from materials like neoprene or silicone, offer excellent sealing performance, flexibility, and durability, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Thermoplastic elastomers, on the other hand, combine the characteristics of rubber and plastics, providing enhanced flexibility, chemical resistance, and recyclability. Both types are essential in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, catering to specific sealing requirements and operational demands.</p></p>

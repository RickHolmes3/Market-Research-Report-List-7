<p><strong>The Insert Automotive Thermostat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Automotive Thermostat</li><li>MAP-Controlled Automotive Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive thermostat market can be divided into two main types: standard automotive thermostats and MAP-controlled automotive thermostats. Standard automotive thermostats regulate engine temperature by allowing coolant flow based on a preset temperature, ensuring optimal engine performance. In contrast, MAP-controlled thermostats adjust coolant flow dynamically using data from the manifold absolute pressure sensor, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. This advanced control allows for improved engine response and performance, making MAP-controlled thermostats increasingly popular in modern vehicles.</p></p>

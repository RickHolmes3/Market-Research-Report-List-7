<p><ul><li>Three Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproof laminate flooring is categorized primarily into two types: three-layer and multilayer. Three-layer laminate features a core layer sandwiched between a top decorative layer and a bottom backing, offering moderate moisture resistance and stability. In contrast, multilayer laminate consists of multiple layers, enhancing durability and water resistance, making it suitable for high-moisture areas. Both types provide a cost-effective flooring solution that mimics the appearance of natural materials while ensuring easy maintenance and longevity in wet environments.</p></p>

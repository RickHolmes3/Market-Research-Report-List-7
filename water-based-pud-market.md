<p><p>The water-based polyurethane dispersion (PUD) market comprises three primary types: polyester, polyether, and polycarbonate. Polyester PUDs are known for their excellent mechanical properties and resistance to abrasion, making them ideal for coatings and adhesives. Polyether PUDs offer superior flexibility and hydrolytic stability, making them suitable for various applications, including textiles and foams. Polycarbonate PUDs provide exceptional hardness and UV resistance, often used in high-performance coatings and sealants. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and performance requirements.</p></p>

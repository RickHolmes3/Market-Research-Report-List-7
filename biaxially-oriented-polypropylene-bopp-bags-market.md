<p><p>The Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) bags market primarily features two production methods: the Flat Film Strength Method and the Tubular Quench Method. The Flat Film Strength Method involves extruding a flat film that is then oriented to enhance strength and clarity, suitable for applications requiring rigidity. In contrast, the Tubular Quench Method creates tubular films through a continuous process, allowing for efficient production of lightweight, versatile bags. Both methods cater to diverse packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wrapping and packaging machines are categorized into horizontal and vertical markets based on their orientation and functionality. Horizontal wrapping machines operate with a side-to-side motion, ideal for larger products or continuous operations, facilitating high-speed packaging. In contrast, vertical machines utilize an up-and-down motion, which is better suited for bulk items or smaller packages, enhancing efficiency in packing liquids and granular goods. Both types serve distinct sectors, optimizing packaging processes tailored to product dimensions and operational needs.</p></p>

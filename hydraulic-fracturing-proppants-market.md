<p><p>The hydraulic fracturing proppants market includes three primary types: frac sand proppants, resin-coated proppants, and ceramic proppants. Frac sand proppants, composed of durable sand, are widely used due to their effectiveness and cost-efficiency. Resin-coated proppants are coated with resin for added strength and reduced fines generation. Ceramic proppants, made from aluminum oxide, offer high strength and conductivity, suitable for high-pressure environments. These proppants enhance well productivity by keeping fractures open during oil and gas extraction.</p></p>

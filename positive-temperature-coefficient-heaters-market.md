PTC air heaters use PTC materials to efficiently warm air for residential and industrial applications. PTC heat conductors convert electrical energy into heat, making them suitable for direct heating in various devices. Convection heaters leverage PTC technology to circulate warm air, providing comfortable heating in enclosed spaces. These heaters offer energy efficiency, safety, and consistent temperature control, driving their popularity in diverse markets.</p></p>

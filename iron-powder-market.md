<p><p>The iron powder market is categorized into types such as atomized iron powder, reduced iron powder, and others. Atomized iron powder, produced by rapidly cooling molten iron, offers uniform particle sizes and high purity, making it suitable for additive manufacturing and metal injection molding. Reduced iron powder is derived from iron oxide through reduction processes, providing high reactivity and is commonly used for producing magnets and as a reducing agent. Other types encompass a variety of specialized powders for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>

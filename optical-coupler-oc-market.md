<p><ul><li>Non-linear</li><li>Linear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Coupler (OC) market is segmented into non-linear and linear categories based on their operational characteristics. Non-linear optical couplers facilitate changes in signal intensity or phase with variations in input conditions, making them ideal for applications like signal processing or switching. In contrast, linear optical couplers maintain a consistent relationship between input and output signals, suitable for functions like signal distribution and amplification. Both types play crucial roles in telecommunications, data centers, and optical networks.</p></p>

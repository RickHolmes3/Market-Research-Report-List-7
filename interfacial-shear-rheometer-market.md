<p><ul><li>Cone Plate Rheometer</li><li>Concentric Cylinder Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Interfacial Shear Rheometer market includes two primary types: cone plate rheometers and concentric cylinder rheometers. Cone plate rheometers provide precise measurements of shear forces at interfaces using a cone-shaped geometry, which minimizes shear gradient and improves accuracy. Concentric cylinder rheometers, on the other hand, measure viscosity by rotating one cylinder inside another, effectively applying shear across a defined gap. Both types are essential for assessing the rheological properties of materials, particularly in industries like cosmetics, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

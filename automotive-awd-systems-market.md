<p><p>The automotive AWD (All-Wheel Drive) systems market can be categorized into two main types: automatic AWD and manual AWD. Automatic AWD systems engage and distribute power to all wheels dynamically, enhancing traction and stability without driver input, ideal for varied driving conditions. Conversely, manual AWD systems allow the driver to switch between 2WD and AWD modes, offering control over power distribution based on specific needs. Both systems cater to different consumer preferences, focusing on convenience versus driver engagement.</p></p>

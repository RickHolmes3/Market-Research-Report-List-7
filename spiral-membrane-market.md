<p><p>The spiral membrane market encompasses various filtration technologies including Reverse Osmosis (RO), Nanofiltration (NF), Ultrafiltration (UF), and Microfiltration (MF). RO utilizes a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved solids, making it suitable for desalination and water purification. NF targets smaller molecules and divalent ions, ideal for water softening and specific industrial processes. UF effectively separates macromolecules and bacteria, while MF focuses on larger particles and suspended solids. These types cater to diverse applications in water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

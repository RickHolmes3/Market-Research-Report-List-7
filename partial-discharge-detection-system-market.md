<p><p>The Partial Discharge Detection System market is categorized into online and offline detection methods. Online detection involves continuous monitoring of electrical equipment during operation, allowing for real-time assessment of partial discharge activity. This method enhances reliability by identifying potential failures instantly. Offline detection, on the other hand, takes place when the equipment is not in service, utilizing techniques like diagnostic testing to assess the condition of insulation and cables. Both methods are crucial for maintaining the integrity and safety of electrical systems.</p></p>

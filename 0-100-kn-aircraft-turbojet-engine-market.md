<p><p>The 0 - 100 kN aircraft turbojet engine market is segmented into axial-flow and centrifugal types. Axial-flow turbojets utilize rotating blades to compress air and achieve high efficiency, making them ideal for high-speed aircraft. They offer better performance at higher altitudes and speeds. In contrast, centrifugal turbojets use a radial compressor to increase airflow pressure, functioning effectively in lower-speed applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs, influencing performance, fuel efficiency, and design requirements in aviation.</p></p>

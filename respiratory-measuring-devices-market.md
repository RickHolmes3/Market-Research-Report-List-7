<p><p>The respiratory measuring devices market encompasses various instruments crucial for assessing respiratory health. Pulse oximeters measure blood oxygen saturation levels non-invasively, providing quick insights into respiratory function. Spirometers evaluate lung functionality by measuring airflow and volumes during breathing. Capnographs monitor carbon dioxide levels in exhaled air, crucial for anesthesia and critical care settings. Peak flow meters gauge peak expiratory flow rates in asthma patients, helping manage their condition effectively. Together, these devices enhance diagnosis and treatment of respiratory disorders.</p></p>

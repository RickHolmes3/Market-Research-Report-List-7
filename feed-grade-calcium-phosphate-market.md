<p><p>The Feed Grade Calcium Phosphate market encompasses several key types: Monocalcium Phosphate (MCP) is primarily used as a digestible phosphorus source in animal feed. Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP) provides both calcium and phosphorus and supports livestock growth. Mono-Dicalcium Phosphate (MDCP) combines the benefits of MCP and DCP, enhancing mineral absorption. Tricalcium Phosphate (TCP) is utilized for its high calcium content. Together, these products cater to the nutritional needs of livestock, promoting health and productivity in animal farming.</p></p>

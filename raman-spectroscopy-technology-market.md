<p><p>Raman Spectroscopy technology encompasses various market types that enhance analytical capabilities. Surface-enhanced Raman scattering (SERS) utilizes rough metal surfaces to amplify Raman signals, allowing for the detection of low-concentration analytes. Tip-enhanced Raman scattering (TERS) combines scanning probe techniques with traditional Raman methods, enabling nanoscale analysis with high spatial resolution. Other methodologies include spatially offset Raman spectroscopy and fiber-optic Raman spectroscopy, catering to diverse applications across pharmaceuticals, biology, and materials science, driving innovation and market growth in spectroscopy solutions.</p></p>

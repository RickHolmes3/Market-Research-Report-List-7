<p><ul><li>Natural</li><li>Synthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic pesticides market comprises two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural pesticides are derived from organic sources, such as plants, minerals, or microorganisms, and are generally perceived as safer and environmentally friendly. They often target specific pests with minimal impact on non-target organisms. Synthetic pesticides, although not derived from natural sources, are designed to mimic natural pest control mechanisms and can offer effective solutions for a broader range of pests. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable farming practices.</p></p>

<p><p>The Single Mechanical Seals market primarily features two types: Pusher Type Seals and Non-Pusher Seals. Pusher Type Seals maintain consistent contact and adapt to changes in the sealing environment, making them suitable for fluctuating pressures and temperatures. In contrast, Non-Pusher Seals are fixed and do not allow for axial movement, providing robustness in stable conditions but less adaptability. Both types serve crucial roles in preventing leakage in various industrial applications, enhancing equipment efficiency and reliability.</p></p>

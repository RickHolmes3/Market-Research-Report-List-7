<p><strong>The Flow Cytometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analytical Flow Cytometer</li><li>Sorting Flow Cytometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow cytometer market is segmented into two primary types: analytical flow cytometers and sorting flow cytometers. Analytical flow cytometers are designed to measure and analyze cellular characteristics, such as size and granularity, providing detailed profiles of cell populations. In contrast, sorting flow cytometers can isolate specific cells from a mixed population based on their properties, allowing for further analysis or experimental use. Both types play crucial roles in research and clinical applications, including immunology, oncology, and diagnostics.</p></p>

<p><p>The sodium malate market consists of two primary types: monosodium malate and disodium malate. Monosodium malate is a single sodium salt of malic acid, often used as a flavor enhancer and acidity regulator in food and beverages. Disodium malate, containing two sodium ions, serves as an effective buffering agent and is commonly found in pharmaceuticals and dietary products. Both types are valued for their functional properties, contributing to the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries.</p></p>

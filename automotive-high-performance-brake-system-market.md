<p><p>The automotive high-performance brake system market primarily comprises disc brakes and drum brakes. Disc brakes utilize a rotating disc and caliper setup, offering superior heat dissipation and stopping power, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, drum brakes consist of a cylindrical drum and shoe assembly, generally providing cost-effectiveness and reliable performance in standard applications. While disc brakes dominate high-performance segments for enhanced reliability and responsiveness, drum brakes continue to see use in specific applications like economy vehicles and light trucks.</p></p>

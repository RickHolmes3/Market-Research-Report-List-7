<p><ul><li>Full-Ring Pet Scanner</li><li>Partial-Ring Pet Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET scanner market is categorized into full-ring and partial-ring systems. Full-ring PET scanners feature a complete circular configuration of detectors, providing enhanced image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for routine clinical use. In contrast, partial-ring PET scanners utilize a segment of detectors, which may result in lower resolution and sensitivity but offer a more compact and cost-effective alternative for specific applications. Each type serves distinct needs in medical imaging, impacting diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>

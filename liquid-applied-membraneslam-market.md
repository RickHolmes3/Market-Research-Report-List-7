<p><p>Liquid Applied Membranes (LAM) are waterproofing solutions used to protect surfaces from moisture and environmental damage. The market can be segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Bituminous** membranes, derived from asphalt, offer excellent waterproofing and durability. </p><p>2. **Elastomeric** membranes provide flexibility and can withstand temperature variations. </p><p>3. **Cementitious** membranes are based on cement and are often used in areas requiring strong adhesion, such as terraces and basements. </p><p>4. **Others** include polymer-based and hybrid systems that combine properties of multiple materials for specialized applications.</p></p>

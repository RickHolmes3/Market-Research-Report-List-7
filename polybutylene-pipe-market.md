<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polybutylene pipe market includes two main types: Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H is characterized by its uniform molecular structure, providing high strength and durability, making it suitable for water distribution systems. In contrast, PB-R features a random arrangement of polymer chains, enhancing flexibility and thermal stability, which is advantageous in applications requiring bendability. Both types serve diverse plumbing and heating applications, contributing to their growing demand in construction and infrastructure sectors.</p></p>

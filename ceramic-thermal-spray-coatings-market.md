<p><p>Ceramic thermal spray coatings are categorized into two primary market types: metal and non-metal. Metal coatings primarily include materials such as tungsten carbide and chromium, which provide enhanced hardness and wear resistance. These coatings are ideal for protecting surfaces in harsh industrial environments. Non-metal coatings, on the other hand, encompass oxides, nitrides, and other ceramic compounds, offering properties like thermal resistance and electrical insulation. Both types are utilized in applications ranging from aerospace to automotive, enhancing performance and durability.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Transformer Oil Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Axial Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transformer Oil Pumps Market consists primarily of two types: centrifugal and axial flow pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize a rotating impeller to increase the velocity of the oil, making them suitable for high-flow applications, ensuring efficient circulation in transformers. In contrast, axial flow pumps move the oil along the axis of the shaft, ideal for situations requiring high flow rates at lower pressures. Both types are essential for maintaining transformer performance, cooling, and prolonging equipment lifespan.</p></p>

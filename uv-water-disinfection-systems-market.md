<p><strong>The UV Water Disinfection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Medium Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV water disinfection systems are categorized into medium pressure and low pressure types based on the intensity of the ultraviolet light they emit. Medium pressure systems utilize high-intensity UV lamps that can effectively treat larger volumes of water quickly, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, low-pressure systems use lower-intensity UV lamps, providing gradual disinfection, ideal for smaller-scale residential or municipal uses. Both types aim to eliminate pathogens without altering water quality, ensuring safe drinking water.</p></p>

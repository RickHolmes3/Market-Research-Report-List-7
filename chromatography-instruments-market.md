<p><p>The chromatography instruments market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Gas Chromatography Instruments** separate volatile compounds using gas as the mobile phase.</p><p>2. **Liquid Chromatography** employs liquid solvents to analyze complex mixtures, ideal for pharmaceuticals and biochemistry.</p><p>3. **Supercritical Fluid Chromatography** utilizes supercritical fluids, offering high efficiency and reduced solvent usage.</p><p>4. **Thin-Layer Chromatography** involves a stationary phase on a flat substrate for quick analysis of small samples.</p><p>5. **Others** encompass emerging technologies and hybrid methods enhancing analysis versatility and sensitivity.</p></p>

<p><p>The GPS vehicle trackers market can be categorized into two primary types: wired and battery-powered. Wired GPS trackers are directly connected to the vehicle’s power system, offering a continuous power supply and often enhanced reliability, making them ideal for fleet management. In contrast, battery-powered GPS trackers are portable and easier to install, as they don’t require wiring. They are suitable for personal use and monitoring various assets, providing flexibility for users who need non-permanent tracking solutions.</p></p>

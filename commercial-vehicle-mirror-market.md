<p><p>The commercial vehicle mirror market is divided into two main types: internal mirrors and external mirrors. Internal mirrors, typically mounted within the vehicle cabin, provide drivers with visibility of passengers and the rear interior space, enhancing safety and comfort. External mirrors, mounted on the vehicle’s exterior, are crucial for providing drivers with visibility of blind spots, road conditions, and surrounding traffic. Both mirror types are essential for optimal vehicle operation and safety in commercial transport applications.</p></p>

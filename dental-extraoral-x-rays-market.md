<p><p>The dental extraoral X-ray market primarily comprises two main types: panoramic and Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT). Panoramic X-rays provide a comprehensive view of the entire mouth, including teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures, making them ideal for assessing oral health and planning treatments. In contrast, CBCT offers 3D imaging, delivering detailed cross-sectional views that enhance diagnostic accuracy for complex cases, such as implant planning and orthodontics. Both types are essential for effective dental diagnostics and treatment strategies.</p></p>

<p><p>The pervious pavement market comprises three primary types: pervious concrete, porous asphalt, and interlocking concrete pavers. Pervious concrete features interconnected voids allowing water to flow through, reducing surface runoff. Porous asphalt offers similar benefits, using a specialized mix to create a permeable surface. Interlocking concrete pavers are designed with gaps that facilitate drainage while providing structural integrity. Each type is utilized for enhancing stormwater management, supporting eco-friendly infrastructure, and improving urban drainage systems while reducing flooding risks.</p></p>

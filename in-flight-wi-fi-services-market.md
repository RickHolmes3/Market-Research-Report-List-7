<p><strong>The In-Flight Wi-Fi Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-to-ground technology</li><li>Satellite technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Flight Wi-Fi services are primarily categorized into two types: air-to-ground technology and satellite technology. Air-to-ground technology relies on a network of ground stations that communicate with aircraft through radio signals, providing cost-effective and high-speed connectivity. In contrast, satellite technology uses orbiting satellites to deliver internet access, ensuring global coverage even over remote areas. While satellite offers broader reach, air-to-ground is often preferred for domestic flights due to its lower operational costs and faster installation.</p></p>

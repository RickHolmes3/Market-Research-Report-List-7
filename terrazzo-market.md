<p><p>The Terrazzo market is primarily divided into two types: Inorganic Terrazzo and Epoxy Terrazzo. Inorganic Terrazzo, made from cement and marble chips, offers a classic, durable surface ideal for high-traffic areas. It is known for its longevity and ability to incorporate various aggregate materials. On the other hand, Epoxy Terrazzo utilizes a resin-based binder, providing a seamless finish with vibrant colors and patterns. This type is more flexible in design and is often favored for modern commercial spaces due to its ease of maintenance.</p></p>

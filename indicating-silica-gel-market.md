<p><p>The Indicating Silica Gel market is primarily divided into two types: Blue Indicating Silica Gel and Orange Indicating Silica Gel. Blue silica gel contains cobalt chloride, which changes from blue to pink when saturated with moisture, providing a clear visual indicator of humidity levels. In contrast, orange silica gel uses a non-toxic indication method, changing from orange to green upon moisture absorption. Both types are widely utilized in packaging, electronics, and pharmaceuticals to protect products from moisture damage.</p></p>

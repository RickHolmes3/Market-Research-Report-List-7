<p><p>The power supply system for electrified railways comprises several market types. The Direct Power Supply System delivers electricity directly from substations to trains, ensuring efficiency and reliability. The BT (Battery-Traction) Power Supply Mode incorporates battery systems to support traction power, enhancing operational flexibility. The AT (Alternating-Traction) Power Supply Mode utilizes alternating current for train propulsion, optimizing energy usage. Additionally, other market types may include hybrid systems or innovative technologies that further improve power distribution and sustainability in railway operations.</p></p>

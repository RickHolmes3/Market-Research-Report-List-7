<p><strong>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline 3D AOI</li><li>Offline 3D AOI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment market consists of two primary types: inline 3D AOI and offline 3D AOI. Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated within the production line, allowing for real-time inspection of PCBs during manufacturing, thus enhancing efficiency and minimizing defects. Conversely, offline 3D AOI systems operate separately from the production line, providing detailed inspection capabilities for batches of PCBs after assembly, enabling comprehensive quality control and analysis before products reach the market.</p></p>

<p><p>Hydraulic accumulators are devices used to store hydraulic energy and maintain system pressure in hydraulic systems. The market is segmented into three main types: Bladder accumulators, which use a flexible bladder to separate the hydraulic fluid from a gas; Piston accumulators, which utilize a movable piston to separate the fluids; and Diaphragm accumulators, featuring a flexible diaphragm to achieve the same function. Each type offers distinct advantages in applications, affecting performance, efficiency, and maintenance characteristics.</p></p>

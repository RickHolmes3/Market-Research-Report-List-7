<p><p>Telematics in the automotive market is categorized into three types: Embedded, Integrated, and Tethered. Embedded systems have built-in hardware and software for connectivity, offering seamless, independent functionality. Integrated systems combine vehicle systems with external devices, often leveraging smartphones for connectivity. Tethered systems rely on external devices, like smartphones, for connectivity, using the vehicle’s display for interface. Each type balances cost, functionality, and user experience, catering to diverse consumer needs and technological advancements in connected vehicles.</p></p>

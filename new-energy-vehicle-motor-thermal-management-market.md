<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Motor Thermal Management market comprises two primary types: air cooling and liquid cooling. Air cooling utilizes ambient air to dissipate heat generated by the motor, offering a simpler and lighter solution, albeit with limited efficiency at high performance. Liquid cooling, on the other hand, employs a coolant fluid circulated through the system to effectively manage higher temperatures, enhancing performance and longevity. Both methods are essential for optimizing NEV performance and ensuring reliable operation in various conditions.</p></p>

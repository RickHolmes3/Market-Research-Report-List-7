<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Self Regenerative Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capsule Self Regenerative Concrete</li><li>Intrinsic Self Regenerative Concrete</li><li>Vascular Self Regenerative Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-regenerative concrete market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Capsule Self-Regenerative Concrete** involves microcapsules containing healing agents that activate upon cracking, releasing materials to repair the damage.</p><p>2. **Intrinsic Self-Regenerative Concrete** incorporates bacteria or chemical agents mixed directly into the concrete, enabling it to automatically heal cracks when they occur.</p><p>3. **Vascular Self-Regenerative Concrete** features a network of vascular channels that transport healing agents to cracks, providing a continuous repair mechanism and enhancing durability.</p></p>

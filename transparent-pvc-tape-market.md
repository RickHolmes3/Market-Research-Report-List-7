<p><ul><li>One Side</li><li>Two-Sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transparent PVC tape market is segmented into two main types: one-sided and two-sided tapes. One-sided tape features adhesive on a single side, making it ideal for general applications like sealing and bonding surfaces. In contrast, two-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, which allows for mounting and attaching items without visible tape. Both types are widely used in industrial, packaging, and household applications, each catering to specific needs based on their adhesion properties and usability.</p></p>

<p><p>The Large Animal Telemetry market encompasses various technologies used for tracking and monitoring wildlife. GPS telemetry utilizes satellite signals to provide precise location data, enabling real-time tracking over extensive areas. Ultrasonic telemetry employs sound waves to determine position and movement, often used in underwater environments or dense vegetation where GPS may be unreliable. Other methods include radio frequency identification (RFID) and cellular-based systems, which offer complementary tracking solutions depending on the specific requirements of the study or habitat.</p></p>

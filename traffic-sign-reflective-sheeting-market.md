<p><p>The Traffic Sign Reflective Sheeting market primarily comprises two types: Glass Bead Type and Micro Prismatic Type. Glass Bead Type sheeting utilizes small glass beads embedded in the surface, providing moderate reflectivity and cost-effectiveness, often used for local roads and basic signage. In contrast, Micro Prismatic Type sheeting employs advanced micro-prismatic technology, enhancing reflectivity and visibility under low light conditions, making it ideal for highways and critical road signs where safety is paramount. Both types facilitate effective traffic management and improve overall road safety.</p></p>
